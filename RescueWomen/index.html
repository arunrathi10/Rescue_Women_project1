Realizing the Crisis

Domestic abuse is a societal disaster rather than merely a personal problem. An annual cycle of terror and violence that impacts not just the victims but also their families, communities, and society at large causes millions of people to suffer in silence. Gender, age, race, and socioeconomic class are important factors in domestic abuse. It causes generational pain, upends lives, and leaves behind wounds that frequently endure a lifetime.

A Ripple Effect

How Domestic Abuse Hurts Us All

The boundaries of a home are not where domestic abuse ends. Higher medical expenses, less productivity, and a burdened social services system are the results. Witnessing abuse increases a child's risk of mental health problems, low academic performance, and involvement in the cycle of violence. More crime, social unrest, and financial losses are the consequences that communities must bear.

Rescue Women International

A Beacon of Hope

We are dedicated to breaking the cycle of domestic abuse. We provide immediate support to victims, offer long-term healing programs, and work tirelessly to prevent future violence through education and advocacy. Our holistic approach addresses the root causes of abuse and empowers survivors to reclaim their lives. But we can't do it alone. We need your support to continue our mission and create a world where everyone feels safe in their own home.
